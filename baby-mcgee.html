A Welcome Letter

To Baby McGee — on the day you arrived

We didn't know your name yet. We didn't know what you'd look like. We didn't know if you'd have your dad's stubbornness or your mom's warmth — though something tells us both. We didn't know anything about you, except that we already loved you completely.

And then you arrived. And everything we didn't know became everything we can't imagine living without.

Your brother Levi has been waiting for you. Phoenix will probably steal your blanket. This family was already full of love — and somehow, you made it fuller.

Welcome home, little one. You were made on purpose, for this family, at this exact moment. That's not nothing. That's everything.

We will spend the rest of our lives making sure you know how wanted you are.

— Mom & Dad 🌿
