Our Story

A Little Miracle
On the Way

Ben and Brittney met on October 21, 2022 at the Buffalo Chip Saloon in Cave Creek, Arizona — and from that night forward, life moved in the most beautiful direction. A proposal at Riverview Park in October 2024 (Bentley famously jumped in the lake to celebrate 🐾), a wedding in Greer, Arizona on November 1, 2025, and two weeks honeymooning in Thailand — life has been one extraordinary chapter after another.

Then came the best surprise of all: by late December 2025, they discovered a new McGee was on the way. Due September 4, 2026 — and they have absolutely no idea if it's a boy or girl.

"A little miracle is on the way — and we'd love to celebrate this season with the people who mean the most to us."

This baby is already surrounded by so much love — big brother Levi, their German Shorthair Pointer Phoenix, and a whole family of people who can't wait to meet them. Come be a part of this story.
