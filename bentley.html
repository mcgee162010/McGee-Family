Bentley

2015  ·  April 9, 2026  ·  10½ years of pure golden goodness

"Brittney's service animal, her shadow, her best friend. Always and forever."

Bentley was a golden retriever in every sense of the word — golden in color, golden in spirit, and golden in the way he made Brittney feel like the most important person in every room he walked into. He was her constant. Her comfort. Her best friend. He served as Brittney's service animal — a role he was born for and carried with quiet, unwavering devotion.

He came into Brittney's life as a puppy — long before Ben, long before Levi — and spent 10 and a half years being exactly what a dog should be: loyal beyond measure, endlessly gentle, and completely devoted to the person he loved. He was there through every season of Brittney's life, and when the time came, he left the same way he lived: peacefully, and surrounded by love.

Bentley passed on April 9, 2026. He was 10 and a half years old. The house is quieter without him. But his paw prints are everywhere — on the floors, on the furniture, and permanently on the hearts of everyone who knew him.
