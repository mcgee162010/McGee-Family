Meet the McGees

Ben
Dad · Husband

He's happiest when the people around him are doing well, the house projects are finished, and there's something good cooking. He's the kind of person who does things on purpose — and the kind of dad Levi can look up to.

🍎 Apple 💪 Fitness 🏡 Home Projects 📊 Data & Systems 🎓 ASU '27
Britt
Mom · Wife

Brittney is the heart of the McGee home — warm, grounded, and steady. She's an incredible mom to Levi, a devoted wife to Ben, and now excitedly preparing to welcome Baby McGee. She loves her animals, her family, and making their house feel like a home.
