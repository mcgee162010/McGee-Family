October 30, 2024
He got down on one knee — and Bentley jumped in the lake
💍 The Proposal

Ben had been planning the proposal for over a year. The ring was in his safe. He had permission from Brittney's father. He'd spent months consulting his best friend Matthias — who happened to be there the night they first met at the Buffalo Chip Saloon — about the perfect moment. The plan: wait for Brittney to get off work, say "let's go for a walk with the dogs," and propose at sunset by the lake at Riverview Park.

At 5:30 PM on the dot, everything was set. Matthias was posted nearby in all black with a camera. Ben had the ring in his pocket. Brittney was venting about her day at work, completely unaware. Then — Bentley spotted the lake, leapt in, and soaked everything. Ben had to yank a dripping golden retriever out of the water with one hand while the other was wrapped around an engagement ring.

He recovered. He grabbed her hand. He told her how much she and Levi meant to him. He got down on one knee. Brittney's eyes went wide — "REALLY… REALLY? YES… YES!!"

And just like that, it was official. Even Bentley couldn't stop it. 🐾💍

November 20, 2022
Sick, nervous, and 15 minutes late — Ben met Levi
🧒 Levi

It was November 20th, 2022. Ben was home sick in bed when his phone rang. Brittney's voice: "I know it's last minute, but IF YOU ARE FREEEeee — you should come to this event with me and meet Levi!"

He had never dated a woman with a child before. Meeting Levi was a big deal — he knew it. But something told him he couldn't miss this. He took an espresso shot, jumped in his truck, and drove to the Enchanted Christmas Event at the Salt River Spring Training Fields in Scottsdale.

Levi was three years old and far more interested in the Christmas lights than in whoever "Ben" was. He ran around full of energy, completely unbothered. But as the night went on, something easy and natural happened. By the end of the evening, Levi was on Ben's shoulders — laughing, running, doing exactly what a three-year-old does. No resistance. No hesitation. Just a little kid who decided Ben was alright.

Ben drove home that night with a quiet confidence. He was exactly where he was supposed to be.

🤠
October 21, 2022
A Bumble match, a hot air balloon, and the Buffalo Chip Saloon
💍 How We Met

It started almost a year before they finally met in person. Early 2022 — both swipe right on Bumble. Brittney makes the first move. Ben, immediately interested, suggests dinner. No response. A double date. She backs out. A hot air balloon ride. Silence. Ben didn't take it personally. He just waited. If it's meant to happen, it will.

On the morning of October 21st, 2022, Ben sent that same hot air balloon suggestion one more time. That same evening — without any planning whatsoever — both Brittney and Ben ended up at the exact same place: The Buffalo Chip Saloon in Cave Creek, Arizona. Music, dancing, a rodeo, and two people who had been circling each other for almost a year.

Ben was standing outside when he saw her — a brunette walking toward him. Then it clicked. As she got closer, she said: "BEN!" And he blurted out without hesitation: "BRITTNEY… YOU ARE SO CUTE!"

They laughed about all the failed attempts. They spent the whole night together. And as the evening ended, Ben said: "No, I'm not texting you. You can text me if you want to meet again."

She texted. And the rest is the McGee family story. 🤠
