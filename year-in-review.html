Annual Letter · December 2026

To Everyone Who's Been Part of This Year

2026 was the year we became official. Ben and Brittney got married on a mountain in Greer, flew to Thailand, came home newlyweds, and found out a baby was on the way — all in the span of about six weeks. If you had told us a year ago where we'd be standing, we might not have believed you.

We lost Bentley in April. That one still hurts in ways we don't always have words for. He was there from the beginning of Brittney's story — long before Ben, long before Levi. He was at the wedding. He jumped in the lake during the proposal. He was the goodest boy until the very end.

But grief and gratitude can coexist. And this year had both in abundance. Levi turned seven on the seventh. Baby McGee is coming. The family is bigger and louder and more full of love than it's ever been.

Thank you for being part of it. For the RSVPs, the prayers, the messages, the presence. Faith. Family. Forever — we mean it.

— Ben, Brittney, Levi & Baby McGee 🌿
Queen Creek, Arizona · 2026
